The Problem We are Solving

Almost every city has a huge migrant population. It has moved places due to personal reasons and mostly due to employment opportunities.

Often, these populations unite in groups and share bonds.

What is common among them is that they maintain the home culture and crave the delicacies they left behind in their hometown.

It is only when someone visits the natives that they bring back the delicacies and share them among their group members.
